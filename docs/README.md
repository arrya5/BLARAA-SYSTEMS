## Document Structure

Every service document follows this structure so you always know what to expect:

1. **Plain English Explanation** — What is the regulation? Why does it exist?
2. **Legal Framework** — Every law, notification, and gazette reference
3. **Who Must Comply** — Exact categories of obligated entities
4. **Government Data Tables** — Complete data from official notifications
5. **Calculation Logic** — Step-by-step formulas with examples
6. **Example Walkthrough** — A real-world scenario computed end-to-end
7. **All Sources** — Every government document cited with URL/reference
8. **Limitations** — What the tool does and does not cover

---

## Key Principle

> Every number in every tool comes from a government notification, gazette, or CERC/BEE order.
> Nothing is assumed or made up. If a number cannot be sourced, it is flagged clearly.

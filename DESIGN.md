# Design System: Eco-Energy Revolution

## Brand Atmosphere
The design should feel environmentally conscious, forward-thinking, and deeply connected to nature and sustainable technology. The vibe is a mix of modern clean-tech and beautiful illustrative landscapes.

## Core Principles
1. **Illustrative & Atmospheric:** Use expansive, colorful vector illustrations featuring nature (mountains, fields) integrated with sustainable technology (wind turbines, solar panels, modern eco-cities).
2. **Clean Typography:** Bold, high-contrast sans-serif typography for headlines to create impact, with highly readable secondary text.
3. **Friendly UI Elements:** Soft, fully rounded (pill-shaped) buttons. Minimalist top navigation.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Action (Buttons, Dark UI):** Deep Navy/Indigo (`#284687`)
- **Primary Brand/Sky:** Vivid Cyan/Sky Blue (`#1db5f2` / `#00b0f0`)
- **Nature Accents:** Forest/Teal Greens (`#194c48`, `#143c3a`), Warm Yellows/Creams for sunlight/fields (`#eecd72`, `#fdf0cb`)
- **Text (on light):** Dark Navy/Slate (`#1a2942`)
- **Text (on dark/images):** Pure White (`#ffffff`)

## Typography
- **Headings:** Poppins, Montserrat, or Inter. Bold, tracking slightly tight.
- **Body:** Inter or Roboto. Regular weight, relaxed line height for readability.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Shape:** Fully rounded / pill-shaped (`border-radius: 9999px`)
- **Style:** Solid background (Deep Navy) with white text. No borders. Subtle hover state.
- **Padding:** Spacious, e.g., 12px vertical, 32px horizontal.

### Navigation
- Top right aligned, plain text links.
- White text with high opacity, fading slightly on hover.
- Clean, no underlines.

### Hero Section
- Edge-to-edge illustration background.
- Left-aligned content block.
- Large, multi-line headline in white.
- Subtitle paragraph below the headline.
- Primary Call to Action button below the subtitle.
- Logo in top left.
